## First Comment (Founder Story)
Hey folks —

PaidUp was the first product I built in this ecosystem. The problem was simple: Stripe Invoicing is great for sending invoices, but does absolutely nothing when they go unpaid. No reminders. No follow-ups. No escalation. Just... silence.

So I built the missing layer.

PaidUp sits on top of Stripe and watches your invoices. When one goes overdue, it kicks off a sequence:

Day 1: Friendly nudge — "Hey, just a reminder your invoice is due"
Day 3: Gentle follow-up — "Wanted to make sure you saw this"
Day 7: Firm reminder — "Invoice is now past due"
Day 14: Escalation — "Please resolve to avoid late fees"
Day 21: Final notice
Day 30: Last attempt

Every email is customizable. Every sequence is configurable. All tracked in a clean dashboard.

PaidUp has a free plan (50 invoices/month) and paid plans at $49/$149. Completely self-serve — connect Stripe, customize your sequence, and let it run.

This was the first brick in the wall. Feedback welcome!

— Scy
